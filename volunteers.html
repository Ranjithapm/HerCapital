Volunteer with Us

Welcome to the Volunteers section of HerCapita! We're thrilled that you're considering contributing your time and expertise to support our mission. By becoming a volunteer, you'll play a crucial role in empowering entrepreneurs and helping them succeed. We offer two types of volunteer opportunities:

1. Mentorship Volunteers

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide one-on-one or group mentorship sessions.
  • Help mentees develop actionable business plans.
  • Offer advice on business challenges and opportunities.
  • Act as a sounding board for new ideas and strategies.

2. Onsite Verification Volunteers

Key Responsibilities:

  • Conduct thorough onsite visits to verify business operations.
  • Document and report findings to our team.
  • Ensure entrepreneurs meet our program criteria.
  • Provide feedback and recommendations for improvement.
